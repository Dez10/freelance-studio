# Client Intake Form Template

**Use this template to create a Google Form for collecting detailed project information from clients.**

---

## Instructions:
1. Go to [Google Forms](https://forms.google.com)
2. Create a new form titled: "Web Design Project - Client Intake Form"
3. Add the following questions/sections:

---

## Section 1: Client Information

**1. Full Name** *(Short answer - Required)*

**2. Business/Brand Name** *(Short answer - Required)*

**3. Email Address** *(Short answer - Required)*

**4. Phone Number** *(Short answer - Required)*

**5. Website URL (if you have one)** *(Short answer - Optional)*

---

## Section 2: Project Details

**6. Which package have you selected?** *(Multiple choice - Required)*
- QuickLaunch ($97-$197)
- Business Build ($250-$500)
- Premium Upgrade ($600-$1,200+)
- Not sure yet / Custom

**7. What type of business/service do you offer?** *(Short answer - Required)*

**8. Who is your target audience/ideal client?** *(Paragraph - Required)*

**9. What is the main goal of your website?** *(Multiple choice - Required)*
- Generate leads/inquiries
- Sell products/services
- Showcase portfolio/work
- Provide information
- Build credibility
- Other

**10. Do you have existing brand materials? (logo, colors, fonts)** *(Multiple choice - Required)*
- Yes, I have a logo and brand guidelines
- I have a logo only
- No, I need help with branding
- I want a brand refresh

---

## Section 3: Website Content

**11. Do you have written content ready? (text for your pages)** *(Multiple choice - Required)*
- Yes, all content is ready
- I have some content
- No, I need help writing content (AI-assisted)

**12. Do you have images/photos to use?** *(Multiple choice - Required)*
- Yes, I have professional photos
- I have some photos but may need more
- No, I need AI-generated or stock images

**13. List the pages you need** *(Paragraph - Required)*
Example: Home, About, Services, Gallery, Contact

**14. Do you need any special features?** *(Checkboxes - Optional)*
- Contact form
- Online booking/scheduling
- Photo gallery
- Menu/price list
- Blog
- Testimonials/reviews
- Social media integration
- Google Maps
- Other (please specify)

---

## Section 4: Design Preferences

**15. Do you have any color preferences?** *(Short answer - Optional)*
Example: Blue and white, earthy tones, bright and modern

**16. What style do you prefer?** *(Multiple choice - Required)*
- Modern and clean
- Bold and colorful
- Elegant and minimal
- Warm and friendly
- Professional and corporate
- Creative and artistic

**17. Are there any websites you like? (share 2-3 URLs for inspiration)** *(Paragraph - Optional)*

**18. Websites you DON'T like (what to avoid)** *(Paragraph - Optional)*

---

## Section 5: Technical Information

**19. Do you own a domain name?** *(Multiple choice - Required)*
- Yes, I have a domain
- No, I need help purchasing one
- Not sure

**20. If yes, what is your domain name?** *(Short answer - Optional)*

**21. Do you have hosting?** *(Multiple choice - Required)*
- Yes, I have hosting
- No, I need hosting recommendations
- Not sure

---

## Section 6: Timeline & Budget

**22. When do you need the website completed?** *(Multiple choice - Required)*
- ASAP (24-48 hours)
- Within 1 week
- Within 2 weeks
- Flexible timeline

**23. What is your budget range?** *(Multiple choice - Required)*
- $97-$197 (QuickLaunch)
- $250-$500 (Business Build)
- $600-$1,200+ (Premium)
- Custom/Negotiable

---

## Section 7: Additional Information

**24. Is there anything else we should know about your project?** *(Paragraph - Optional)*

**25. How did you hear about us?** *(Multiple choice - Optional)*
- Google search
- Social media
- Referral from friend/colleague
- Online ad
- Other

---

## After Creating the Form:

1. **Set up email notifications** so you receive submissions instantly
2. **Create a response** that thanks them and confirms you'll be in touch within 24 hours
3. **Save responses to Google Sheets** for easy tracking
4. **Share the link** with clients after your discovery call

---

**Your Google Form link will be:**
`https://forms.gle/[your-unique-id]`

Save this link and send it to qualified clients after your initial conversation!
